Building production systems that actually ship — from internal enterprise SaaS to consumer apps powered by machine learning.

Keanu is a full stack developer and AI/ML engineer currently building and maintaining proprietary SaaS platforms for California Closets as Business Analyst and Full Stack Developer — including a fully automated inventory system with barcode scanning, UPC generation, QuickBooks financial sync (C#), and a delivery workflow platform coordinating teams across the entire installation pipeline.

Beyond his current role, Keanu co-founded and serves as Director of Technology (remote) at Tiny Titans Sports — a youth athletics startup (soccer & basketball, ages 2–8) that he helped pioneer from zero to $60K+ in revenue in its first year, building the full web presence and operational infrastructure from the ground up. The organization is an MLS GO partner based in Atlanta.

On the product side, Keanu has shipped ChurnShield — a full-stack ML SaaS (Next.js + FastAPI + scikit-learn) that predicts customer exit risk from a CSV upload — and LifeQuest, a gamified productivity app that turns daily habits into an RPG. Both are live and deployed on Vercel.

His technical stack spans Next.js, React, Node.js, Python, FastAPI, scikit-learn, SQL, Tableau, C#, and REST APIs. He approaches every project as both engineer and analyst — writing the code and understanding the business problem it solves.
